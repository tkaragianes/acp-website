What we do

The Technology team is responsible for managing creek data and publicizing the Arcade Creek Project through informational videos and this website. Tech is organized into 3 subdivisions: Data, Web, and Video.

The Data group is responsible for Dashboard, a portal through which students and teachers can monitor their participation in the Arcade Creek Project. They input hours from every student in the project, manage attendance at lectures and conservation outings, and organize a new list of student participants every year.

The Web group is responsible for maintaining this website with new information as it arrives.

The Video group is responsible for producing informational media, such as instructional videos for each study and direction videos to each site. They also film lectures and take pictures at outings.

How we do it

Technology follows modern practices in website maintenance and video production. By clicking on the button, you can view the most recent version of the protocol.
