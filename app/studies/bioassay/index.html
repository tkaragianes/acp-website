What we do

Biological assay assesses the relative toxicity of creek water by calculating the survival rate of the indicator species Ceriodaphnia dubia over 48 hours. Our rigorous protocol, modeled after the EPA guidelines, involves testing reference toxicants/Wasser water controls, performing pH/conductivity tests, and peer checking to ensure the validity of our data.

We would like to thank the Chemistry study for their continued support in providing us with water samples from the creek.

How we do it
